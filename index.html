About

Monocurl is custom-designed scripting language in conjunction with a desktop application that allows you to create videos and slideshow presentations using math. In particular, it is targetted towards STEM explanatory content and related fields. The core idea of Monocurl is to combine the benefits of programmatic animations with the traditional feel of a video editor. Continue reading
