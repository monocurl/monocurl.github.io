2. Meshes in Monocurl

We'll now talk about meshes. Meshes and animations are the two biggest types. This article is a bit long, but is important, so we recommend thorough reading.

2.1 Why meshes?

Monocurl operates on a point-set model. Also, everything is a mesh. This is a difference. By having everything be one type, you lose some benefits, but the uniformity makes things pretty understandable.

2.2 Mesh-Trees

Mesh-trees. We define a mesh-tree recursively. A mesh is considerd a mesh-tree. A vector of mesh-trees is considered a mesh-tree. In general, I recommend you make no assumptions about the structure of your mesh-tree. Most library functions are structure invariant. However, the advantage of a mesh-tree is that it's very easy to add to a mesh-tree
